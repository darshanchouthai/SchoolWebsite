The History Of Oxford Maths Group Of Education

Nalatavada:
  Nalatavada: With a dream of providing quality education, the Oxford (Mutts) Education Institution, led by B.G. Math, has become one of the most renowned educational institutions in the district. For the past two decades, it has been offering excellent and disciplined education to rural students. Situated in the scenic lap of nature in a hilly region, the institution has gained immense popularity for its outstanding educational services and has been instrumental in shaping the future of thousands of students.

Establishment and Growth:
  Oxford (Mutts) Institution was founded in the small village of Nagarabetta in Muddebihal Taluk during the 1996-97 academic year as a training center for Sainik, Navodaya, and Kittur schools with just 33 students. Beginning as a training center, the institution expanded by 2003-04, launching the Oxford Higher Primary and S.D.K. Kannada and English Medium High Schools. Over the years, it has gained prominence through its high-quality education, enlightening the lives of thousands of students.

State-Level Achievements:
  For the past three consecutive years, the institution's high school students have secured the 2nd, 3rd, and 4th ranks at the state level, bringing recognition across the entire state. Over 4,000 students who studied at the high school up to SSLC level have secured government jobs based on merit, securing a bright future. Additionally, thousands of students have achieved prestigious positions in the state due to the strong academic foundation provided by the institution.

Science College Expansion:
  Following strong requests from parents to extend education beyond high school, the Oxford (Mutts) management launched the Expert PU Science College in the 2015-16 academic year. Every year, the institution has been achieving excellent results, preparing rural students for medical and technical courses and helping them secure admissions to top colleges across the state. Through this, the institution has gained the trust and confidence of people across Karnataka.

ನಮ್ಮ ಗುರಿ

  ಗ್ರಾಮೀಣ ಭಾಗದ ಮಕ್ಕಳಲ್ಲಿ ಹೆಚ್ಚಿನ ಪ್ರತಿಭೆ ಇರುತ್ತದೆ. ಅದನ್ನು ನಾವು ಗುರುತಿಸುವ ಕಾರ್ಯ ಮಾಡುತಿದ್ದೇವೆ. ಗ್ರಾಮೀಣ ಮಕ್ಕಳು ಕೂಡ ವೈದ್ಯರಾಗಬೇಕು ಎಂಬ ಕನಸು ನನ್ನದಾಗಿದೆ. ಆ ನಿಟ್ಟಿನಲ್ಲಿ ನಮ್ಮ ಸಂಸ್ಥೆಯಲ್ಲಿ ಉತ್ತಮ ಶಿಕ್ಷಣಕ್ಕೆ ಹೆಚ್ಚು ಪ್ರಾಮುಖ್ಯತೆ ನೀಡುತ್ತೇವೆ. ಇದರಿಂದಾಗಿಯೇ ರಾಜ್ಯದ ಮೂಲೆ ಮೂಲೆಯಿಂದ ಪಾಲಕರು ತಮ್ಮ ಮಕ್ಕಳನ್ನು ನಮ್ಮ ಸಂಸ್ಥೆಯಲ್ಲಿ ಪ್ರವೇಶ ನೀಡಿಸುತ್ತಾರೆ. ಪಾಲಕರ ಕಂಡAತಹ ಕನಸನ್ನು ನಾವು ಪೂರೈಸಲು ಹಗಲಿರುಳು ಶ್ರಮಿಸುತ್ತೇವೆ.
